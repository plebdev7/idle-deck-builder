## Theme Overview

### Core Concept
You are an apprentice mage defending a magical tower from endless waves of invaders. Your deck represents your arsenal of spells and magical abilities. As you master different schools of elemental magic, you unlock more powerful spells and can defend against increasingly dangerous threats.

### Narrative Framework
- **Setting:** A mage tower under constant siege
- **Role:** Apprentice mage learning to harness elemental magic
- **Goal:** Defend the tower, master all elements, become a legendary archmage
- **Conflict:** Waves of invaders attacking the tower
- **Progression:** Learning new elements, discovering advanced magical techniques

### Design Philosophy
- **Strategy over Story:** Theme provides context but doesn't interfere with gameplay
- **Clarity over Uniqueness:** Familiar fantasy elements let players focus on mechanics
- **Abstract Visuals:** Color coding and simple shapes convey information efficiently
- **Functional Naming:** All names clearly communicate mechanical purpose

## Element System

### Arcane (Base Tier)
**Color Scheme:** Gray/Silver  
**Concept:** Fundamental magic that underlies all elemental schools  
**Themes:** Universal magical theory, raw magical energy, foundation of all spellcasting  
**Persistence:** Always relevant because specialized magic builds on arcane foundations

### Fire
**Color Scheme:** Red/Orange  
**Concept:** Aggressive, destructive magic  
**Themes:** Burning, explosions, aggression, damage over time  
**Typical Card Types:** High damage attacks, DoT effects, resource destruction

### Water
**Color Scheme:** Blue  
**Concept:** Flowing, adaptive magic  
**Themes:** Waves, currents, healing, control, adaptation  
**Typical Card Types:** Healing, resource generation, card draw, enemy control

### Earth
**Color Scheme:** Green/Brown  
**Concept:** Defensive, enduring magic  
**Themes:** Stone, growth, stability, protection, persistence  
**Typical Card Types:** Defense, shields, resource accumulation, steady effects

### Air
**Color Scheme:** Yellow/White/Cyan  
**Concept:** Swift, unpredictable magic  
**Themes:** Wind, lightning, speed, mobility, randomness  
**Typical Card Types:** Fast attacks, card cycling, tempo plays, multi-target effects

## Class System

### Class Rarity Structure

**Common Classes: Apprentices (2 tiers each)**
- Starting classes found in Arcane packs
- Each specializes in one element + Arcane foundation
- Simple, clear paths for learning game mechanics

**Rare Classes: Adepts (3 tiers)**
- Found in Arcane packs (rare drops) and colored tier packs
- Multi-element paths
- Advanced strategic options

**Epic Classes: Masters (4 tiers)**
- Found in colored tier packs (rare drops)
- Hybrid element paths
- Powerful synergies

**Legendary Classes: Archmages (5 tiers)**
- Found in special/event packs
- Complex multi-element paths
- Unique mechanics and bonuses

## Terminology Guide

### Core Terms
- **Essence:** Magical energy currency
- **Tier:** Level of magical mastery (Arcane, Fire, Water, Earth, Air)
- **Deck:** Your collection of active spells
- **Pack:** Collection of new spell cards
- **Class:** Magical specialization path

### Card Terms
- **Spell/Card:** Interchangeable - the magical abilities in your deck
- **Attack/ATK:** Offensive power
- **Defense/DEF:** Protective power
- **Generate:** Produce Essence
- **Trigger:** Activate an effect
- **Synergy:** Cards that work well together

### Gameplay Terms
- **Wave:** A combat encounter against invaders
- **Tower:** Your defensive position (the thing you're protecting)
- **Invaders:** Enemies attacking your tower
- **Mastery:** Progression in a magical school
- **Transcendence:** Prestige mechanic (reset with bonuses)

## Naming Conventions

### Card Naming Pattern
`[Adjective] [Element] [Noun]`

**Examples:**
- Blazing Fire Strike
- Flowing Water Shield
- Crushing Earth Wall
- Swift Air Blade
- Ancient Arcane Bolt

**Alternatives:**
- `[Element] [Type]`: Fire Strike, Water Shield
- `[Descriptive Name]`: Inferno, Tsunami, Earthquake, Hurricane

### Pack Naming
- **Arcane Pack** (basic/common)
- **Fire Pack**, **Water Pack**, etc.
- **Elemental Pack** (mixed elements)
- **Master's Cache** (rare/epic focus)
- **Archmage's Tome** (legendary focus)

### Class Card Naming
- **Common:** `[Element] Apprentice`
- **Rare:** `[Specialty] Adept` or `[Specialty]mancer`
- **Epic:** `[Specialty] Master` or `[Specialty]lord`
- **Legendary:** `[Unique Title]` (Primalist, Void Weaver, etc.)

## Narrative Context (Minimal)

### Background (Optional Flavor)
*The tower has stood for centuries, a beacon of magical learning and power. But the barriers between worlds have weakened, and now creatures from beyond assault the tower day and night. As an apprentice, you must master the elemental arts to defend the tower and discover what lies behind the endless invasion.*

### Progression Narrative
- **Early Game:** Learning basic arcane magic, choosing first elemental specialization
- **Mid Game:** Mastering your chosen element, discovering new magical paths
- **Late Game:** Combining multiple elements, achieving archmage status
- **Endgame:** Transcending mortal limits, exploring forbidden magics

### Combat Context
- **Why constant combat?** The invasion never stops
- **Why auto-battle?** Your tower's defenses work automatically
- **Why deck building matters?** You're programming your tower's defensive spells
- **Why prestige?** Transcend and rebuild from a higher plane of understanding

## Integration with Game Systems

### How Theme Supports Mechanics

**Tier System:**
- Arcane = foundational magic everyone needs
- Colored tiers = specialized elemental schools
- Multi-tier classes = combining elemental knowledge
- Cross-tier synergies = mixing magical schools

**Resource Generation:**
- Essence = magical energy extracted from your spells
- Generator cards = spells that produce essence as side effect
- Resource accumulation = building up magical reserves

**Combat:**
- Auto-battle = tower defenses work automatically
- Deck composition = how you've configured your defensive spells
- Victory = successfully defended a wave
- Defeat = tower breached (potential failure condition)

**Card Collection:**
- Packs = tomes of spells
- Duplicates = studying same spell multiple times (experience)
- Leveling cards = mastering specific spells
- Rare cards = discovering lost or advanced magic

**Prestige:**
- Transcendence = achieving higher plane of existence
- Reset = returning to mortal form but keeping knowledge
- Prestige currency = crystallized wisdom/power
- Permanent bonuses = enlightened understanding
